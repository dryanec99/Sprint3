About Us

Welcome to Community Fridge! We are a dedicated group of university students who are passionate about making a positive impact in our community.

As part of our group project, we have developed this platform to connect donors, food recipients, and volunteers to reduce food waste and support those in need. Our project reflects our commitment to social responsibility, sustainability, and innovation.

Through this collaborative effort, we aim to create a transparent, efficient, and supportive system that empowers everyone to contribute to a better community. Thank you for joining us on this journey. Together, we can make a real difference!

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us and where we're headed

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and food insecurity in our community by creating an accessible platform that connects food donors with recipients and volunteers.

We strive to make food sharing simple, dignified, and efficient while fostering a sense of community responsibility and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no food goes to waste while people go hungry. A community where sharing resources is normalized and technology serves as a bridge to connect those with excess to those in need.

We aim to expand our model to communities worldwide, creating a global network of local food sharing initiatives.
