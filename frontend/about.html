About Us

Welcome to Community Fridge! We are a dedicated group of university students who are passionate about making a positive impact in our community. As part of our group project, we have developed this platform to connect donors, food recipients, and volunteers to reduce food waste and support those in need.

Our project reflects our commitment to social responsibility, sustainability, and innovation. Through this collaborative effort, we aim to create a transparent, efficient, and supportive system that empowers everyone to contribute to a better community.

Thank you for joining us on this journey. Together, we can make a real difference!
