Donation Guidelines

Welcome to the Community Fridge Donation Guidelines page. Please follow these recommendations to ensure your food donations are safe, nutritious, and beneficial to our community.

  • Food Safety: Ensure all food items are fresh, properly stored, and within their expiration dates.
  • Packaging: Package food securely to avoid spoilage or contamination during transportation.
  • Perishable vs. Non-Perishable: Clearly label perishable items, and prioritize non-perishable items when possible.
  • Accepted Items: Donate fruits, vegetables, dairy products, canned goods, and other nutritious options. Avoid items that are expired or improperly stored.
  • Donor Responsibilities: Follow local health standards and guidelines, and keep a record of your donations for tracking purposes.
  • Contact Information: Provide accurate contact details so we can reach out if additional information is required.

If you have any questions regarding these guidelines or need further assistance, please contact our support team.
